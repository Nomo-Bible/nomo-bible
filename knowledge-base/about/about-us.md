# About Nomomartyria Bible Platform

## Through the Law and the Testimony, Christ is Revealed

Nomomartyria Bible Platform was created from a simple conviction: the Bible is its own best interpreter.

This project is the result of more than a decade of personal Bible study, research, and careful comparison of Scripture with Scripture. Rather than approaching the Bible through changing opinions or traditions, Nomomartyria is built upon the conviction that God's Word forms a unified, harmonious revelation from Genesis to Revelation.

The platform is designed to encourage careful, thoughtful study. Every tool is intended to help the reader examine the biblical text more deeply—from Strong's Concordance and cross-references to study notes, topical research, and original-language resources. The goal is not merely to find information, but to understand how the whole of Scripture reveals Jesus Christ.

The name *Nomomartyria* comes from the Greek words for "Law" (*nomos*) and "Testimony" (*martyria*), reflecting the biblical principle found in Isaiah 8:20:

> "To the law and to the testimony: if they speak not according to this word, it is because there is no light in them." (Isaiah 8:20, KJV)

Our guiding motto is:

**Per Legem et Testimonium, Christus Revelatur.**

*"Through the Law and the Testimony, Christ is revealed."*

This platform has been developed independently with the desire to provide a comprehensive Bible study environment that is accessible to anyone seeking to study God's Word. Wherever possible, it uses public-domain biblical resources and carefully respects the intellectual property rights of licensed works.

Nomomartyria is not intended to replace personal Bible study, prayer, or the guidance of the Holy Spirit. Rather, it exists to assist students of Scripture by bringing together trusted research tools in a single, organized workspace.

Every feature is developed with one purpose in mind:

To help readers discover the beauty, harmony, and consistency of God's Word and to see Christ more clearly through the Scriptures.

Thank you for visiting Nomomartyria Bible Platform. It is my sincere hope that this resource will encourage deeper study, strengthen faith, and inspire a greater love for the Word of God.

> "Search the scriptures; for in them ye think ye have eternal life: and they are they which testify of me." — John 5:39 (KJV)

## About the Founder

Nomomartyria Bible Platform was founded by **Dennis Hardy**, an independent Bible student with more than a decade of dedicated study focused on the harmony of Scripture. His work emphasizes interpreting the Bible through the Bible itself, with particular attention to the relationship between the Law of God and the Testimony of Jesus as complementary witnesses that reveal the character and mission of Christ.

While the platform reflects years of research and development, it remains a growing project. New study tools, reference works, and educational resources continue to be added with the goal of providing a rich, Christ-centered environment for serious Bible study.
