# Project Governance

## Purpose

The purpose of this document is to establish the authority, stewardship, responsibilities, and development process governing the Nomomartyria Bible Platform.

The platform is not merely a software project. It is a long-term ministry dedicated to revealing Jesus Christ through the faithful study of Scripture. Every contributor—whether human or artificial intelligence—serves this mission within clearly defined responsibilities.

---

# Governing Principle

Artificial intelligence exists to assist the project.

It does not define the project.

The authority of the platform belongs to its human stewards.

No artificial intelligence system shall establish theology, doctrine, philosophy, mission, governance, or educational direction.

---
