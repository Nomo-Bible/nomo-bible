# Architecture

## Purpose

This document defines the overall architecture of the Nomomartyria Bible Platform.

The architecture is designed to preserve biblical knowledge independently of any particular software technology while providing a scalable foundation for future applications, websites, mobile devices, APIs, educational systems, printed publications, and research tools.

The architecture exists to serve the Knowledge Base.

The Knowledge Base does not exist to serve the architecture.

---

# Architectural Philosophy

The Nomomartyria Bible Platform consists of three primary layers.

Knowledge

↓

Services

↓

Presentation

The Knowledge Base stores truth.

The Services organize and deliver truth.

The Presentation Layer allows people to study truth.

Each layer has a distinct responsibility.

---

# Layer One

## The Knowledge Layer

The Knowledge Layer is the permanent repository of biblical knowledge.

It contains:

- Bible Text
- Study Notes
- Cross References
- Doctrine Articles
- Topics
- Word Studies
- Character Profiles
- Place Profiles
- Event Profiles
- Prophecy Studies
- Sanctuary Studies
- Charts
- Timelines
- Maps
- Educational Courses
- Media Resources

This layer changes slowly.

It is the intellectual heart of the platform.

---

# Layer Two

## The Service Layer

The Service Layer provides access to the Knowledge Base.

Examples include:

- Search
- Cross-reference engine
- Scripture lookup
- Topic lookup
- Doctrine lookup
- Relationship engine
- User accounts
- Authentication
- Reading plans
- Bookmark management
- Personal notes
- API services
- Optional AI services

This layer contains business logic.

It never owns the knowledge.

---

# Layer Three

## The Presentation Layer

The Presentation Layer provides interfaces through which people interact with the Knowledge Base.

Examples include:

- Website
- Mobile applications
- Tablet applications
- Desktop applications
- Printed publications
- Educational curriculum
- Future interfaces

Presentation may change.

Knowledge remains.

---

# Primary Platform Modules

Version One of the Nomomartyria Bible Platform shall consist of the following major modules.

## Bible Reader

The central reading environment.

Every study begins here.

---

## Knowledge Explorer

Allows users to discover connected biblical knowledge.

Every verse should lead naturally to deeper study.

---

## Study Notes

Verse-by-verse educational commentary.

---

## Cross Reference Explorer

Displays biblical relationships between passages.

---

## Doctrine Library

The organized collection of doctrinal studies.

---

## Prophecy Explorer

Historical and prophetic studies centered upon Scripture.

---

## Sanctuary Explorer

Studies related to the biblical sanctuary and Christ's ministry.

---

## Word Study System

Original-language studies connected directly to Scripture.

---

## Academy

Courses, lessons, quizzes, examinations, and structured learning.

---

## Research Library

Long-form articles, charts, diagrams, historical resources, and educational publications.

---

## User Workspace

Private user content.

Examples include:

- Notes
- Highlights
- Bookmarks
- Reading plans
- Collections
- Study journals

User content remains completely separate from Canonical Content.

---

## Administration

The management system used to maintain the Knowledge Base.

Only authorized editors may modify Canonical Content.

---

# Relationship-Centered Architecture

Every major module communicates through the Knowledge Base.

No module owns biblical knowledge.

Instead, every module retrieves information from the canonical repository.

This architecture ensures consistency across every future application.

---

# Scalability

The architecture shall support future expansion without requiring major redesign.

Future additions may include:

- Additional Bible translations
- Additional languages
- Mobile applications
- Desktop applications
- Public APIs
- Church resources
- Ministry tools
- Printed study Bible editions
- AI-assisted research tools

These additions should integrate naturally into the existing architecture.

---

# Architectural Principles

The architecture shall always strive to be:

- Modular
- Maintainable
- Scalable
- Extensible
- Well documented
- Relationship-centered
- Knowledge-first
- Scripture-centered
- Christ-centered

---

# Closing Statement

The architecture of the Nomomartyria Bible Platform exists for one purpose:

To preserve, organize, and present biblical knowledge in a manner that faithfully reveals Jesus Christ through the inspired Scriptures.

Knowledge remains.

Software evolves.

Christ is revealed.
