# Bible Data

## Purpose

This document defines how the biblical text is represented within the Nomomartyria Bible Platform.

The Bible is the foundational source of all knowledge within the platform.

Every study note, doctrine, cross reference, chart, article, timeline, and educational resource ultimately connects back to the biblical text.

For this reason, the biblical text serves as the primary anchor of the entire Knowledge Base.

---

# Canonical Text

Version 1 of the Nomomartyria Bible Platform shall use the Public Domain King James Version (KJV).

The Bible text shall remain completely separate from study notes, commentary, doctrinal material, and user-generated content.

The inspired text is never modified.

All supplemental material is attached to the text without becoming part of it.

---

# Canonical Structure

The Bible consists of:

- Testament
- Book
- Chapter
- Verse

Every verse possesses a permanent identity.

Examples:

Genesis 1:1

John 3:16

Revelation 22:21

These references become the primary anchors used throughout the platform.

---

# Verse as the Primary Knowledge Object

Within the Nomomartyria Bible Platform, every verse is treated as a primary Knowledge Object.

Every verse may connect to numerous related resources, including:

- Study Notes
- Cross References
- Doctrine Articles
- Topics
- Word Studies
- Character Studies
- Place Studies
- Event Studies
- Sanctuary Studies
- Prophecy Studies
- Charts
- Timelines
- Maps
- Educational Courses
- Research Articles
- User Notes
- Bookmarks

The biblical text remains the center of these relationships.

---

# Separation of Content

The platform maintains a strict distinction between:

Inspired Scripture

and

Supplemental educational material.

Study notes are not Scripture.

Cross references are not Scripture.

Doctrine articles are not Scripture.

Artificial intelligence is not Scripture.

The biblical text remains visually and structurally distinct from every other content type.

---

# Metadata

Each biblical verse may contain metadata describing information such as:

- Testament
- Book
- Chapter
- Verse
- Book abbreviation
- Literary genre
- Traditional author
- Historical period
- Related themes
- Related topics
- Cross-reference count
- Study-note availability
- Original-language links

Metadata assists navigation and research.

It does not alter the biblical text.

---

# Relationships

Every verse functions as the center of an expanding network of biblical knowledge.

Rather than existing as isolated text, verses become connected to every relevant Knowledge Object throughout the platform.

This relationship-centered model enables deeper study while preserving the integrity of Scripture.

---

# Future Bible Versions

The platform architecture shall allow additional Bible translations to be incorporated in the future.

Each translation shall remain independent while sharing the same underlying Knowledge Base and relationship model.

Version 1 of the platform shall focus exclusively on the Public Domain King James Version.

---

# Design Principle

The biblical text is immutable.

Knowledge grows around it.

The text remains fixed.

The understanding expands.

---

# Closing Statement

Every feature of the Nomomartyria Bible Platform ultimately begins and ends with the inspired Scriptures.

The Bible is not merely one component of the platform.

It is the foundation upon which the entire Knowledge Base is built.
