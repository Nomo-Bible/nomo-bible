# The Nomomartyria Knowledge Base

## Purpose

The Nomomartyria Knowledge Base is the canonical repository of all biblical knowledge developed and maintained by the Nomomartyria Bible Platform.

It serves as the single authoritative source from which every application, website, mobile application, printed publication, educational course, API, research tool, and future technology derives its information.

The Knowledge Base exists independently of any specific software implementation. Software may change over time, but the Knowledge Base shall remain the permanent repository of the ministry's biblical research, educational resources, and theological work.

---

# Mission

The mission of the Knowledge Base is to preserve, organize, connect, and present biblical knowledge in a manner that reveals Jesus Christ through the harmonious witness of Scripture.

Rather than functioning as a digital library of isolated documents, the Knowledge Base is designed as a living network of interconnected biblical knowledge.

Every item within the Knowledge Base should help the student discover deeper relationships within Scripture.

---

# Foundational Principle

Every item stored within the Knowledge Base is considered a Knowledge Object.

A Knowledge Object is any piece of information that possesses meaning, purpose, and relationships to other biblical knowledge.

Knowledge is not stored as isolated information.

Knowledge is stored as connected understanding.

# Relationship-Centered Design

The primary value of the Knowledge Base is not the documents themselves.

Its value is the relationships between them.

Every Knowledge Object should be capable of connecting to other related Knowledge Objects.

These relationships form an expanding network that enables deeper biblical study and richer understanding.

---

# Canonical Content

Canonical Content consists of material officially developed, reviewed, and approved by the Nomomartyria Bible Platform.

Canonical Content forms the permanent educational foundation of the platform.

---

# User Content

User-generated material is maintained separately from Canonical Content.

Examples include:

- Personal notes
- Highlights
- Bookmarks
- Study journals
- Reading plans
- Collections

User content never alters Canonical Content.

---

# Single Source of Truth

The Knowledge Base shall function as the single source of truth for every Nomomartyria application.

No application shall maintain independent theological content outside the Knowledge Base.

All websites, APIs, mobile applications, AI tools, printed publications, and educational resources shall derive their knowledge from this common repository.

---

# Long-Term Vision

The Knowledge Base is intended to become one of the world's most comprehensive repositories of Christ-centered biblical knowledge.

Every future feature of the Nomomartyria Bible Platform shall strengthen, expand, and preserve this repository while remaining faithful to Scripture and to the governing principles established by the platform.

Knowledge is the product.

Software is the delivery system.

Christ is the center.
